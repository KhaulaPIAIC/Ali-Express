How Buyer Protection works

  1. Place your order and pay securely.
  2. Your payment is held securely until you receive your order.
  3. Once satisfied, confirm receipt and release your payment.
  4. If there's an issue, contact the seller and try to resolve it.
  5. If you're still not happy, escalate the issue to us.
  6. We'll investigate and mediate the dispute.
  7. You'll get a fair resolution, up to a full refund.
